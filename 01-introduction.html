Types of Needs

  • Normative needs - those based on expert opinion
  • Felt needs - community reactions to a problem or issue
  • Expressed needs - actionable beliefs
  • Comparative needs - those that are relative to similar institutions

Four Basic Roles of Libraries

  1. Acquiring materials deemed valuable or useful
  2. Storing and preserving those materials
  3. Organizing materials for efficient access
  4. Providing assistance to individuals in locating information
